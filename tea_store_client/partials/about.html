About Us

It all started in the winter of 2013 with a simple, bold idea: make a better tea store.
But, not just a better tea store – a better way of doing things.
A new kind of tech company that works hand in hand with users to do something amazing, something meaningful. Believing that great products raise people’s living standards and spread happiness,
we bring technology back to the community by building an open and collaborative platform.
Fame and profits alone seldom equal respect.
Respect comes from doing right things the right way and building something that matters
